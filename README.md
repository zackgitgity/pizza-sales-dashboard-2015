## Executive Summary  
This report presents insights extracted from a comprehensive pizza sales dataset. It explores total revenue, order behaviour, ingredient popularity, product performance, and time-based trends. The goal is to support strategic decision-making through data-driven insights.

---

## Overall Sales Performance  
- **Total Revenue:** \$817.86K  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 50K  
- **Total Orders:** 21K  
- **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.3  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** \$38.31  

### Key Observations  
- Sales peaks in **mid-July** and **late November**.  
- Weekend surges especially on **Fridayâ€“Saturday evenings (5â€“8 PM)**.  
- Minor dips after peaks reflect post-promotion slowdowns.

### Likely Influencing Factors  
- Seasonal impacts (school holidays, Black Friday).  
- Weekend dining behavior.  
- Weather or supply conditions.  
- Operational downtime or reduced hours.

## Product Performance  
Top insights from product-level comparison:

- **Revenue Leaders:** Thai Chicken, Barbecue Chicken  
- **Quantity Leaders:** Classic Deluxe, Hawaiian  
- **Low Performers:** Brie Carre, Mediterranean (low revenue & popularity)  

---

## Size & Ingredient Analysis  
- **Medium and Large pizzas** drive the most sales and revenue.  
- Popular ingredients: **Bacon**, **Chicken**  
- Least popular: **Kalamata Olives**, **Eggplant**  
- Trend suggests customers prefer meat-based toppings.

---

## Correlation Insights  
- Higher-priced pizzas sell in **lower volume** but contribute **higher revenue per order**.  
- Ingredient popularity aligns strongly with revenue contribution.

---

## Order Frequency & Timing  
- **62%** of orders are single-item.  
- **38%** are multi-item.  
- Average interval between orders: **11 minutes**.  
- Peak traffic occurs during **evenings and weekends**.

---

## Recommendations  

### **1. Product Strategy**  
- Promote high-performing pizzas (Classic Deluxe, Thai Chicken).  
- Rework or discontinue underperformers (Brie Carre, Mediterranean).

### **2. Ingredient Optimization**  
- Stock more of high-demand ingredients (Bacon, Chicken).  
- Reduce low-demand items to minimize waste.

### **3. Pricing Strategy**  
- Maintain premium pricing.  
- Add bundle discounts to increase average order quantity.

### **4. Marketing**  
- Launch weekend-focused promotions.  
- Create family bundles or limited-time specials for Fridayâ€“Sunday.

### **5. Operations**  
- Align staff schedules with weekend & evening peaks.  
- Consider midweek promotions to stabilize low-volume periods.

### **6. Customer Experience**  
- Gather targeted feedback on vegetarian pizzas.  
- Improve or redesign vegetarian options.
